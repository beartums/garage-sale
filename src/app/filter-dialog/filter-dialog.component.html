Categories

Click/tap on a grey category to add it to the filters. Once added, you will ONLY see items that belongs to that category. If you click on the now-purple category, it will turn red which means you will ONLY see items that do NOT belong to that category. Click on the 'x' to remove the category from the filters all together.
